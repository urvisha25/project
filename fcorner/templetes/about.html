Farm Corner Services

Farm Corner Services is increase easy access to farm mechanization solutions through rental of tractors and farm equipment for small and large farms.

Small and marginal farmers, who hold more than 80% of the land holdings in India may not be able to afford ownership of tractors or implements.

Farm Corner Services bridges this gap by connecting these farmers with tractor and equipment owners through its Equipmentholder-2-Farmer platform.
